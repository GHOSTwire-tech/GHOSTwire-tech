## ⚡ What is GHOSTWIRE?

GHOSTWIRE is a Telegram-based Solana sniper bot that monitors channels and automatically executes trades when contract addresses are posted. Built for speed, simplicity, and the Solana community.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔒 Private Channel Sniping
Monitor and snipe from private Telegram groups by connecting your account via TG Auth. Your session is encrypted and used only to read messages from channels you add.

### 💼 Multi-Wallet Support
Create up to 5 trading wallets, each with custom buy amounts, slippage, and take-profit settings. Assign different wallets to different channels.

### 🎯 Smart Auto-Snipe
Automatically detect and buy tokens as soon as contract addresses appear in your monitored channels. Deduplication ensures you never buy the same token twice.

### 🤖 Auto-Sell
Set take-profit percentages and market cap targets. The bot monitors your positions and sells automatically when conditions are met.

### 👤 Sender Filter
Filter messages by username. Only snipe calls from specific users like group owners or admins. No more buying random spam.

### 📊 Portfolio Tracking
Track your positions with real-time P&L. View performance by channel, wallet, or individual token. Know exactly where your profits come from.

### 💰 Referral System
Share your referral link and earn 20% of trading fees from everyone you refer — forever. Hold GHOSTWIRE tokens to unlock and claim your earnings.
